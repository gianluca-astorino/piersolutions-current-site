Gianluca Astorino

Who is Gianluca Astorino?

Gianluca Astorino is the Founder of Indera Technologies. As a business oriented, and tech driven individual, Gianluca leads Indera Technologies in the development of all new, and existing solutions for clients.

He has experience as an IBM software developer, experience working with large teams, as well as individual projects where he is developing high level web applications for clients around the globe. He also holds past business experience running e-commerce and media businesses, worked in technically advanced automation for robotics roles, and years of technical experience coding and engaging in web applications.

Location: Toronto, ON

Role: Founder





Indera Foundation


It has always been a goal of mine to create a foundation that allows me to help people around the world, and give back to the community that allowed me to create a business in the first place. That is why we pledge 1% of our revenue to go towards helping the homeless in our home city, Toronto, and children in Africa.
