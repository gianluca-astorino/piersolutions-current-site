Our Story

Hi! My name is Gianluca Astorino, I am the Founder here at Indera Technologies. You've probably already read it on our home page, but we at Indera Tech take pride in the fact that we offer unique tech services to clients globally.

So now the big question remains, why did I start this company. Well, I always had a passion for tech, and my love of computers strands from when I was a kid. As I started coding, I gave web development a shot, and well, it was love at first site. I realized how much I love being able to design, and develop something from scratch for others, and have my work be seen and used by thousands of people, and how people trust me to create something that represents their business and their passion. It's truly rewarding for me.

Since I founded Indera Technologies back in early 2020, we have developed an almost uncountable number of websites, web apps, and other software solutions for businesses around the globe. We have had the honor of working with Enterprise sized clients, Social Media Giants, some awesome athletes and entrepreneurs, small and medium sized businesses, and startups. All of our developers have dozens of years of experience, and have created hundreds of sites on their own, which they use their skills and expertise to design, and develop your sites! We now have a fantastic team of Sales Associates, Developers, and Partner Organizations around the globe.
