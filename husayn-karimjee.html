Who is Husayn Karimjee?

Husayn Karimjee is an Executive Sales Associate at Indera Technologies. As well as being a Sales Associate, Husayn has experience working at BMO, where he researched, assessed, and wrote credit approval proposals to the BMO Capital Markets credit committee for private and publicly traded corporate clients with >$100.0 million CAD in credit facilities.

Husayn also acted as a market maker for retail and commercial trades of up to ~$10,000,000 USD, by pricing currency pairs and booking trades in a fast-paced, detail-oriented environment. He also worked at the Valsoft Corporation, as well as Tefler Capital Fund (UofOttawa), TMX Group, and Investa Insights.

With his experience in his financial and market roles at BMO, and various other organizations, Husayn is avid about taking on this sales role, working with clients, and is valuable part of our team.
